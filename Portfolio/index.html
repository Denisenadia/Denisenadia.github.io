Bargain Travel - Website Portfolio

 

Bargain Travel, the site developed for this project is a budget travel site. The purpose of the website is to provide up to date information on how to explore the world and not spend a fortune. The website will contain information about budget destinations around the world, travel resources regarding flights, accomodation, trip planning, as well as diverse travel gear reviews, from cameras and accessories, to bags and suitcases, appliances and even family-friendly items.

 As target audience, Bargain Travel is aimed at anyone that wishes to discover the world and spend less, which will mostly be working class individuals and families, especially youngsters. From a business perspective, the website is also aimed at travel bloggers, vloggers, influencers and travel gear manufacturers or sellers , as Bargain Travel will feature budget travel articles and vlogs, review and promote diverse travel gear to generate income.

The developments of internet technology suggest that we live in a digital society, where the digital communication has taken over due to its instant aspect and effectiveness. Changes in individuals' habits, for example, online shopping, social media networking, streaming, and more, have forced most institutions and businesses around the world to transition to digital. The travel industry has changed dramatically, the Internet turning it into one of the biggest industries worldwide. Thanks to the Internet, people are able to travel anywhere in the world, and book holidays/trips from their home. People are able to see photographs and watch videos online from places around the world that they want to visit, read about travel experiences on blog websites,  stay up to date with the latest travel news. All these changes have created business opportunities for individuals passionate about travel. Bargain Travel website's creation has been influenced by the increasing popularity of low-budget travel, and because the availability of so much different information online could be overwhelming, the website is focusing only on the low-budget travel  niche, to allow individuals to find information related to this specific type of travel only. The income generation approach has also been influenced by nowadays trends, where collaborations with influencers has become a great digital marketing tool, to reach audience, gain exposure and generate greater revenues.                                                

 

 

Design 

 

A website's design has a significant impact on how the audience appreciates a brands, service, product, etc. A good  design attracts visitors and potential customers. The vision for Bargain Travel is an eye-catching design, but simple at the same time to provide a good user experience, helping visitors to navigate with ease. Also, the design chosen for the website is responsive, which nowadays, adapting the design to accomodate different screen sizes, resolutions and orientations  is a crucial factor.
